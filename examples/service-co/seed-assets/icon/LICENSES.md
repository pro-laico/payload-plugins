# Bundled sample icons

These SVGs are bundled only as **seed/demo data** for the two seeded icon sets. All are solid,
fill-based glyphs (the payload-icons optimizer themes fills to `currentColor` — stroke-based
icons don't theme).

**Base set** (`architecture.svg` … `map-pin.svg`): demo glyphs bundled with this example.

**Alternate set** (`*-alt.svg`): public-domain icons from [the Noun Project](https://thenounproject.com)
(no attribution required; creators credited here as a courtesy):
